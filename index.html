About


I am a licensed counselor in the state of Washington with a Master's degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. I began my career in Chicago, where I worked in schools, hospitals and community settings providing counseling services to children and adults. Now back in my home state, I provide counseling to children, adolescents and adults who may be struggling with depression, anxiety, trauma or relational issues. I have found that children and adults who are provided a safe, welcoming and consistent environment will be better equipped to develop healthy coping skills to help overcome obstacles in their lives. It is my goal to provide that environment for my clients — children and adults alike — so they can find the health and well-being they deserve.
